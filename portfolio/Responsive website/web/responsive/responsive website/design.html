Typography

I chose this kind of typography because it was used a lot in the Pop Art movement. Especially this font called Pop Art Comic, which was, apparently, used in comic books, was also very common in the art itself, the most known are paintings of Roy Lichtenstein, who used comic book style as a pattern for his own art.
